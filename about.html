About Symantix

Symantix is a cybersecurity awareness and protection platform designed to educate users, promote safe digital practices, and provide practical tools for everyday security.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To enable a safer digital ecosystem through awareness, accessible tools, and community engagement.

Vision: A digitally empowered society where citizens and organizations understand and mitigate cyber risks.

Objectives

  • Deliver easy-to-use security tools like password generators, MFA utilities, and secure storage.
  • Provide bite-sized learning resources and quizzes to build cyber hygiene habits.
  • Promote reporting of cyber incidents through official helplines and portals.
